BAYOMA:
Bayesian Operational Modal Analysis

Identify modal properties of civil structures based on Bayesian fomulation

About

Modal properties mainly includes natrual frequencies, damping ratios and modes shapes. Knowing these properties is often the first step to be perfomed in strcutural vibration control and health monitoring, providing a factual basis for further decision making. Operational modal analysis focuses on identifying modal properties based on 'output-only' ambient vibration data. It has attracted great attention in both field test applications and theory development as it can be conducted econimically with the structure under working conditions without artificial loading.

This project focuses on fundamental theory, computational algorithm and practical implementation of Bayesian approach in operational modal analysis.
